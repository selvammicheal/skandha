Mantras that make Upresidence a Land of Pure Bliss

On - Campus Education

Upresidence takes pride in hosting the prestigious Velammal Bodhi Campus within its bounds. An integral part of "The Velammal International School, embodies the vision set by its founders. The Bodhi Campus stands as a testament to academic excellence and holistic development. With exclusive campuses for all age groups, Upresidence ensures that educational aspirations become an intrinsic part of the community's journey towards a brighter future.

Clean Green Living

A Harmonious coexistence of modern living and nature's embrace. Rooted in a commitment to environmental consciousness, the project is meticulously adorned by 50 different trees, creating a pollution-free haven amidst the bustling town of Thiruvallur. Beyond aesthetics – it's a promise to nurture a sustainable lifestyle. Thus, weaving a green tapestry that not only enhances the beauty of the surroundings but also contributes to a healthier ecosystem.

Multigenerational Bliss

Welcome to a home where happiness knows no age – a space where every family member finds their favorite corner to bask in joy.. While tiny tots can frolic in well-designed play areas, elders can find solace in serene spots designed to enrich their lifestyle. With exclusive amenities in each zone, Upresidence ensures that every moment is an opportunity for cherished family memories.

Children's Play Area

A joyful sanctuary for young explorers. The children's play area at Upresidence is not just a space, it's a vibrant and safe haven where laughter echoes, curiosity thrives and friendships blossom. This carefully designed fun zone is dedicated to providing children with an environment to explore, learn, and play freely. From interactive play structures to imaginative corners, every element is crafted to foster a sense of community among young families.

Outdoor Sports Arena

Setting forth for an active lifestyle with dedicated spaces for badminton, tennis, volleyball, basketball, an amphitheater and more. Upresidence transforms the concept of a sports facility into a dynamic space where fitness meets leisure, fostering a sense of community spirit. Whether you're a sports enthusiast or a spectator, this is where the thrill of competition converges with the joy of communal celebration.

Nature’s Retreat

Embark on a journey of natural beauty at Upresidence. Here, the soulful embrace of a Miyawaki forest invites residents to immerse themselves in a lush green haven. Accompanied by a carefully curated walking and jogging track, this park is a retreat for those seeking a peaceful escape amidst the verdant landscape

Top-notch Security

At Upresidence, we prioritize your safety, creating a secure haven where you can live, relax, and enjoy the amenities with peace of mind. The entire project is fully compounded, providing a secure boundary, while our dedicated 24x7 security team ensures constant vigilance. CCTV cameras are strategically placed across the project, forming an additional layer of protection.
